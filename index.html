Le Marais!

Welcome to Le Marais, the heartbeat of Parisian culture and charm! Nestled in the heart of the City of Light, Le Marais invites you to wander through its cobbled streets lined with historic buildings, boutique shops, and quaint cafes. Discover the rich tapestry of French history as you explore landmarks like the stunning Place des Vosges or delve into the vibrant Jewish quarter. Indulge your senses with the eclectic mix of gourmet eateries and bustling markets, offering a taste of Parisian culinary delights.

Top Attractions

  1. Visit the Marché des Enfants Rouges

    Discover Marché des Enfants Rouges for diverse international cuisines and fresh produce in Paris.

  2. Picnic at Place Des Vosges

    Enjoy a picturesque picnic at Place des Vosges, Paris' oldest square, amidst historic beauty and serene surroundings.

  3. Pompidou Centre

    Explore Paris's Pompidou Centre for an immersion into modern art, architecture, and culture.

  4. Brunch at Le Ju’

    Brunch at Le Ju' offers a delightful experience with a creative menu and inviting atmosphere, perfect for enjoying a relaxing meal in Paris.

  5. Falafel at L’as Du Falafel

    L'As du Fallafel is renowned for its exceptional falafel, widely considered among the best in Paris, with a crispy texture and flavorful taste

  • LGBTQ+ Friendly

    Embrace Le Marais' status as Paris' historic LGBTQ+ district by exploring its welcoming bars, clubs, and cafes, as well as landmarks like the LGBTQ+ Center and the annual Pride Parade route.

  • Hidden Courtyards

    Seek out the neighborhood's hidden courtyards and passages, such as Courtyard of the Hôtel de Soubise or Passage du Grand Cerf, which offer tranquil escapes from the bustling streets and reveal the area's architectural charm.

  • Street Art Exploration

    Take a self-guided tour of Le Marais' vibrant street art scene, which includes murals, graffiti, and installations by local and international artists, adding an extra layer of creativity to the neighborhood's streetscape.

  • Watch Out for Pickpockets

    While exploring Le Marais, be mindful of your belongings and surroundings, especially in crowded areas and tourist hotspots, to minimize the risk of pickpocketing incidents.

  • Relax in Parks

    Take a break from the hustle and bustle of the city by spending some time in one of Le Marais' charming parks, such as Square du Temple or Jardin Anne-Frank, perfect for picnics, leisurely strolls, or simply soaking up the sun.

Hidden Speakeasies

  • Little Red Door

    Little Red Door offers a discreet entrance and a cozy, intimate atmosphere reminiscent of clandestine drinking establishments. Known for its innovative cocktails and elegant setting, it's a popular spot for those seeking a sophisticated drinking experience in Le Marais.

  • Moonshiner

    Hidden behind a fake freezer door in a bustling pizza restaurant called Pizzeria Da Vito, Moonshiner offers a speakeasy experience with dim lighting, vintage decor, and expertly crafted cocktails. It's a cozy and atmospheric spot for enjoying drinks away from the hustle and bustle of the streets above.

  • Candaleria

    A hidden gem known for its inventive cocktails and cozy atmosphere, with a secret taqueria in the back.

  • Lavomatic

    Concealed behind the facade of a laundromat, Lavomatic offers a playful and secretive ambiance akin to a speakeasy. Guests enter through a hidden door disguised as a washing machine, revealing a stylish cocktail bar with inventive drinks and a laid-back atmosphere, perfect for a unique night out in Le Marais.

  • Serpent A Plume

    Experience the allure of secrecy and sophistication at Serpent à la Plume, a hidden speakeasy in Le Marais, offering meticulously crafted cocktails and vintage-inspired ambiance.
